## Our Philosophy on AI

**Business Value First**
We start with the business problem, not the technology. AI should be a tool to achieve specific outcomes, not a solution looking for a problem.

**Honesty About Limitations**
AI isn't magic. We'll be direct about what's achievable, what risks exist, and what ongoing investment is required to maintain AI systems.

**Production Quality**
Demo code and production systems are different things. We build systems designed for reliability, maintainability, and operational visibility.

**Security by Design**
AI systems handle sensitive data and make consequential decisions. Security, privacy, and appropriate human oversight are core design considerations, not afterthoughts.
